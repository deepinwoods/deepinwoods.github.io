About Us

Enjoy your stay.

Discovering the hidden wonders of himalayan regions, landed up in a beautiful meadow called the “Jibhi Valley”, we offer a serene haven for those seeking to discover the hidden wonders of the region. Our restful cottages provide clear shots of majestic mountains and are surrounded by lush apple orchards, immersing you in the beauty of nature. Join us on walking tours to nearby villages, connect with local culture, and unwind under the twinkling night sky full of stars. Deep In Woods invites you to experience the magic of the Himalayas and create unforgettable memories amidst nature's vibrant tapestry.
